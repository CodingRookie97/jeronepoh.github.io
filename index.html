Introduction

I am currently a full-time Full Stack Developer working for NTUC LearningHub, having successfully completed the Bachelor Degree of Engineering with Honours in Information and Communications Technology (Software Engineering) at Singapore Institute of Technology (SIT). Initially being specialised in the field of native Android Application (Java) Development, I was tasked to build on an Android application on Remote Assistance Platform during Polytechnic Internship. Utilising my application development expertise acquired from Polytechnic, I had attained the Minister for Defence Award 2018, helping my unit to develop the Maintenance Standard Android Application (MSA) to view the maintenance steps on the go. Since my conversion to full-time with NTUC LearningHub, I had further diversified my role as a backend Java developer having acquired the knowledge to develop Spring Boot applications. My speciality in Java language had also benefited me in acquiring the Oracle Java Foundations Associate Certificate: 1Z0-811. Besides Java, I am also able to program multitude of programming languages which includes C, C++, CSS, HTML, Javascript, PHP, SQL, Python, Kotlin. As a keen learner, I am willing to step out of my comfort zone to learn new technologies and frameworks, adopting good industry practices in the everchanging IT industry.
